James caused many, many crashes along the way, but Matthew seemed rather good for a beginner. Best NT activity ever!

Matthew looked like he had seen death. We visited MIM Co-op, then caught the next train to Great Malvern via Wozza, filming all the way. Filmed more stuff at Great Malvern then caught the train to Hereford. Met Matthew's uncle Nick and and his grandmother in Hereford station, then drove off. We waited ages for fish and chips, then went back to Matthew's uncle's house to eat them. Met Matthew's aunt Sarah and Nelson the dog who growled when he was happy. We had a nice meal, before flooring it back to the station. Caught the train back, and saw a TfW train on the way!

We had a very long conversation on the way back, before transferring to the RRB at Wozza. We had it mostly to ourselves, so ranted about politics for 2 hours - fun. #RRBTime! We showed up in to Oxford at half past midnight, and Matthew cycled around Oxford with a camera for 25 minutes in the pouring rain, whilst James hopped from shelter to shelter - NT Smooth Jazz will be coming soon! We went back to James' room, made up the second bed for Matthew, then talked about Ffynone until stupid o’clock in the morning. Woke up tired.

Matthew went to the Queens Lane Café (oldest in Europe) and had Turkish Coffee, Turkish Delight and a Bagel, before meeting James, crossing the street and going to the Grand Café (Iacobus Iudaecus) for cream tea. We returned to the station, filmed more shit (including NT-phile), then went back to Oxford and Teddy Hall for the last time.
