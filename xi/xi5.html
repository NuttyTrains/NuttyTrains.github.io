Back then we still had ambition, so the plan was to try to tick off every branch line in the Thames valley... despite for some reason starting the day at 16:30. The entire English upper class was in attendance at Henley that Sunday, but in Henley they had an entire town to spread out within. Fortunately for us, we decided to avoid the crowds and head immediately east, 1 stop down the line to Terry May town - Maidenhead.
