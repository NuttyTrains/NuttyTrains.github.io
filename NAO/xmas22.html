The day started bright and early for James, and less so for Matthew, who was dying. The goal on this adventure was to make maximum usage of our completely fucked tickets (which essentially allowed us unlimited travel in Kent for an entire weekend, which is truly crazy), and to make it to Dover by the end of the evening. What could go wrong?

Well it turns out that James' train to Paddington was the first thing to go wrong. It meant that the adventure itself did not exactly start bright and early - James arrived at Imperial at just gone 10am, to find Matthew looking like a ghost dressed in a ushanka. Matthew had a particularly spicy form of death and was genuinely not feeling good - it is actually a wonder that he survived 2 days in the freezing cold: the things we used to do for content when we were still young and energetic is quite astonishing.

The first order of the day was to head to St Pancras International, caffeinate Matthew, feed James, and catch the highspeed train! It was HS1 time, which was very exciting.
