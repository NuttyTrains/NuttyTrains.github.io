About Us

Trains

We are self pronounced train addicts. James, after a trip to London on a HST and only having been on a train once before, was hooked. He was happy to see the introduction of the IET Class 800s and sad to see the end of the Pacers. Before he knew it he was mourning the loss of Arriva Trains Wales, Ave Arriva! Matthew, it was after his many adventures off on the trains and the call of the valleys, it was accentuated by seeing trains everyday going past at college, soon enough, he knew the patterns and told the time by what train went by and questioned descrepencies in the train timetable. Matthew is also incredibly lazy, and may or may not have copied and pasted this entire section from the main page. Fast forward a few months, and James is busy pottering about on his Cornish valley lines (it's a pity trains don't stop at his home station on a Wednesday), meanwhile Matthew is zipping about on the Tube every day, and actually lives next to the Central line. We both study maths degrees, in Exeter and Imperial respectively. The two of us met in Year 9 in secondary school, and bonded during ICT and Latin lessons there. Our precursors to NT began in Year 10, but it took until upper sixth form for the real adventures to start. We look forward to many adventures in future. Ave Arriva!
